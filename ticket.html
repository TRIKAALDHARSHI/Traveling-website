FAQ

What are the advantages of online train booking ?

There are many advantages of online bus ticket booking. You can choose your preferred seats. Online bus ticket booking keeps you away from the long queues of the offline ticket counters. You can view plenty of buses and choose an appropriate bus for your travel considering the amenities, reviews, ratings and bus images available. You can choose the preferred bus type (Volvo Bus, AC or Non AC) and also pickup and dropping point and timings. Your tickets can be booked at a reasonable price with multiple payment options.

Can we book Intrastate railway bookings

Yes, almost all fleets of state road transport corporation buses will be enabled from IRCTC. For example, UPSRTC, APSRTC, GSRTC, OSRTC, Kerala RTC, etc.

Do I need to create an account on DesiTraveller to book tickets

DesiTraveller login credentials can be used for booking the bus tickets. In case, customer does not have IRCTC login he can book through guest user login by providing required email id and mobile no.

Can I cancel my train tickets any time

Yes, you can. Cancellations can only be done online for online bookings through www.bus.irctc.co.in.

What are the payments options ?

We accept all payment options to complete your booking like Debit card, Credit card, Net banking, Wallets and UPI.

How many passengers can be booked in one transactions

Maximum 6 passengers can be booked in one transaction.

Is the name chane or gender change permissable

No, name change or gender change is not allowed. In case of changes, it is to be cancelled and go for fresh booking.

Is ID proof required for booking

No. However, it is mandatory to carry one govt. id card having the same name on the ticket.

Can partial cancellation be done

Yes, partial cancellation of passengers is allowed.

Can we change / modify dates after booking

No. In such cases booking needs to be cancelled and to be rebooked. Cancellation charges as per policy shall levy.
